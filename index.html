How to create a more sustainable product?

Design choice #1
USE PETROLEUM-BASED VIRGIN PLASTIC
Petroleum based virgin plastic depletes non-renewable resources. Try again!
USE RECYCLED PLASTIC
good choice!
Using recycled plastic does not deplete non-renewable resources and enables an effective end of life reusage of plastic.
Design choice #2
ADD RECYCLED PLASTIC
Adding recycled material is an excellent solution, congratulations! But if we inform consumers, they are empowered to make a responsible choice.
Try adding a label…
ADD RECYCLED PLASTIC + ECO-LABEL ON PACKAGING
BENEFITS INCREASED!
An eco-label alerts shoppers to the sustainability of the product so they’re empowered with the right information when making purchases.
Design choice #3
PRODUCE COMPONENTS IN-HOUSE
OPTIMIZED!
By producing components in the same factory, you reduce transportation impact on the environment.
SOURCE COMPONENTS WHERE CHEAPER
Oh no!
This could generate greater transportation needs if the source is far from the manufacturing facility.
Design choice #4
MAKE THE PENCIL REFILLABLE WITH 4 REFILLS INCLUDED
SCORE INCREASED!
Product is designed to be longer-lasting and has a longer length of use right from the start. Smart choice!
MAKE THE PENCIL REFILLABLE
Good start!
However, providing refills with the pencil increases its length of use.
